About Me

Personal Statement

While growing up I spent a lot of time with my computer trying to explore things. It always made me curious about how games or different applications are made.

When I was in grade 9, I developed my interest in website designing, from the internet I got to know where to start. I started to learn HTML from youtube channels among them worth mentioning is the 'Traversy Media' also I acquired much from the book "HTML & CSS" by Jon Duckett. Once I felt comfortable using HTML and CSS I went on to study javascript mainly from the book "Smarter Way to learn Javascript" by Mark Myers. In my free time, I would practice what I learned from different resources.

Time passed and my interests got deeper in web technologies. After finishing one thing I would hop on to the other, I continued to grasp knowledge about different web technologies, I learned some frameworks like Materialize CSS and React JS. My dedication to self-improvement in web design and development has inspired me to study web at a university, where I hope to incorporate new ideas with my knowledge practically and beneficially, as well as to add work to a future portfolio.

I am currently studying BSCS at Fast National University to gain more knowledge about the web and computer. Hopefully, in upcoming semesters, I would be given the chance to deeply study about web designing and I will also gain the ability to address my own personal development needs.
